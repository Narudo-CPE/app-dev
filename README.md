# ⚔️ Lenard's Pick: Kimetsu No Yaiba

## 🌊 The Vibe
After his family is brutally slaughtered and his sister is turned into a demon, Tanjiro Kamado undergoes extreme training to master Sun Breathing and join the Demon Slayer Corps to defeat the Demon King, Muzan.

---

## 💡 Why It's a Must-Watch
1. **Peak Animation:** The visuals provided by Ufotable are so immersive they feel like a whole new level of artistry.
2. **Iconic Soundtracks:** Legendary tracks like *Gurenge* by LiSA perfectly capture the intensity of the series.
3. **Legendary Characters:** Zenitsu's "sleeper" strength and Tanjiro's rapid development make every episode satisfying.
4. **Fast-Paced Growth:** The MC's progression through extreme training is inspiring and high-stakes.

## 🏙️ The Vibe
A perfect mix of action, humor, emotional moments, and coming-of-age struggles — it follows an ordinary teenager, Peter Parker, as he learns that being a hero comes with sacrifice and tough choices.

---

## 💡 Why It's a Must-Watch
